This project focuses on five key business problems identified in the hotel booking dataset. Each problem represents a real business challenge that hotels face, and our database + AI agent provides analytical solutions for them.

## **1. Booking Cancellations**

**Goal:** Identify which customer groups are more likely to cancel their bookings and determine the factors that contribute to higher cancellation rates.
This helps hotels reduce lost revenue and optimize overbooking strategies.

---

## **2. Hotel Occupancy by Month**

**Goal:** Determine which months have the highest and lowest booking volumes and compare occupancy trends between the **City Hotel** and **Resort Hotel**.
This supports seasonal pricing, resource allocation, and marketing planning.

---

## **3. Popular Room Types**

**Goal:** Analyze which room types are booked most frequently and how preferences vary by customer type and season.
The hotel can use this insight for room pricing, renovation priorities, and inventory management.

---

## **4. Revenue by Market Segment**

**Goal:** Evaluate which market segments (Corporate, Direct, Online, Offline, etc.) generate the highest revenue.
This helps identify the most profitable customer acquisition channels and optimize marketing budgets.

---

## **5. Impact of Meal Plans on Bookings**

**Goal:** Determine which meal plans (BB, HB, FB, SC) are in highest demand, and how meal choices relate to customer types and booking behavior.
Hotels can adjust their meal offerings and improve bundled pricing strategies.
