Business Problems

1. Booking Cancellations
Identify which types of customers are more likely to cancel their bookings and what factors contribute to higher cancellation rates.
2. Hotel Occupancy by Month
Determine which months have higher booking volumes and compare occupancy trends between the City Hotel and the Resort Hotel.
3. Popular Room Types
Analyze which room types are booked most frequently and how this varies by customer type and season.
4. Revenue by Market Segment
Evaluate which market segments (e.g., Corporate, Direct, Online) generate the highest revenue for the hotel.
5. Impact of Meal Plans on Bookings
Understand which meal plans (BB, HB, FB, SC) are in highest demand and how meal preferences relate to customer types and booking behavior.
